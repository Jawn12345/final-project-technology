Who can challenge Steam?

Steam holds approximately 75% of the market share for PC games.

They have very, very few competitors. A few notable ones are GOG, Origin, and the Epic Games Store.

The Epic Game Store was created to rival Steam. Epic Games takes a 12% split as opposed to Steamâ€™s 30% split. If the game is made with Unreal Engine, owned by Epic Games, then it is further lowered to 5%.

Epic Games is trying to challenge the monopoly that Steam holds over the distribution of PC Games by offering developers a better profit margin in order to lure developers away from Steam and have their games remain as Epic exclusives.
